Our Story

Welcome to
Villa Velha

Nestled in the charming heart of Alvor, Villa Velha is a celebration of flavour, craftsmanship, and coastal warmth. With spectacular ratings on Restaurant Guru and glowing praise across TripAdvisor, this is where passion meets Portuguese charm.


Chef Alberto Punset brings over 26 years of international experience spanning Spain, London, Sweden, and Switzerland — including time in Michelin-recognised kitchens — to create a menu that fuses the finest Atlantic produce with bold European imagination.


Whether you're celebrating a milestone or simply craving something extraordinary, Villa Velha welcomes you as part of the family.
